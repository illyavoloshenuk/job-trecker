Why this site exists

When you apply to many jobs, it becomes hard to remember where you applied, what happened next, and what you want to follow up on. This site gives you one calm place for all of that.

Save each application Keep job title, company, date, location, salary, contact name, link, and notes together.
See your progress Update the application status as things change, so you always know what is active.
Search later Use filters to quickly find an old application when you need it again.

How it works

The idea is simple. First you create an account, then you save your job applications, and later you come back to update, search, or review them.

Step 1 — Create your account Register with username, email, and password to get your own private dashboard.
Step 2 — Add applications Create entries for every company you applied to and keep useful details in notes.
Step 3 — Filter and manage Later you can filter by status, company, title, or date range to find exactly what you need.
